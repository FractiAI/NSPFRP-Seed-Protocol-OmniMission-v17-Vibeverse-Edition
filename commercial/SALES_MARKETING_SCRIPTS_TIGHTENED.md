## 2. DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPTS

### 2.1 Discovery Call Framework (15-30 minutes)

**Structure:**
1. **Establish Rapport** (2 min)
2. **Understand Current State** (5 min)
3. **Identify Pain Points** (3 min)
4. **Show Solution** (10 min)
5. **Next Steps** (5 min)

---

### 2.2 Opening (2 minutes)

```
"[First Name], thanks for taking the time.

Before I jump in - did you get a chance to try the sandbox?"

[If YES]: 
"Great - what stood out? What questions came up?"
[Listen, take notes]
```

---

### 2.3 Understanding Current State (5 minutes)

**Questions (Pick 3-5 based on context):**

```
1. "Walk me through how you handle [specific process] today."

2. "What's working well with your current setup?"

3. "If you could wave a magic wand and fix one thing, what would it be?"

4. "What have you tried before? What happened?"

5. "What's the cost of not solving this - in time, money, or opportunity?"

6. "Who else is impacted by this problem?"

7. "On a scale of 1-10, how urgent is solving this?"

8. "What happens if you don't solve this in the next [90 days]?"
```

**Key Listening Points:**
- Pain level (1-10)
- Budget authority (can they buy?)
- Timeline urgency
- Current alternatives
- Decision criteria

**Framework: Reflect Back What You Heard**

## 9. SCRIPT OPTIMIZATION RULES

### 9.1 Trapper Grammar Principles

**Always:**
- Lead with pain or desire
- Use specificity (numbers, timeframes, outcomes)
- Create urgency (authentic, not fake)
- Offer proof (demo, test, trial)
- Make it easy (one-click, no friction)
- Respect autonomy ("your call", "no pressure")

**Never:**
- Use superlatives without proof ("best", "perfect")
- Make claims you can't back up
- Pressure manipulatively
- Complicate the offer
- Hide the price
- Ignore objections

### 9.2 98% Sweetspot Strategy

**The Formula:**
```
Value Clarity (25%) + 
Proof Accessibility (25%) + 
Friction Removal (25%) + 
Authentic Urgency (23%) = 
98% Conversion Probability
```

**In Practice:**
- **Value Clarity**: They understand exactly what they get
- **Proof**: They can test it immediately
- **Friction**: No barriers (free sandbox, one-click)
- **Urgency**: Real deadline (Day 36), real consequence (10-30x price increase)

### 9.3 BBHE Optimization

**Tag every script with:**
- **Shell Layer** (which audience: 1-7)
- **Awareness Level** (prospect readiness: 0-100%)
- **Queen Association** (which personality matches)
- **Outcome Intent** (awareness/consideration/decision)

**Adjust language for shell:**
- Shell 7 (Interfaces): Visual, simple, immediate
- Shell 4 (AI): Technical, specific, proof-heavy
- Shell 1 (Blockchain): Deep, complex, comprehensive
